✦ HOW TO PLAY ✦

🎯 Goal

Route the signal from the green Source (S) to every red Target (T) by placing conductive nodes on the grid.

🛠️ Placing Nodes

Select a tool from the palette below the grid, then tap any cell to place that node. Tap a filled cell to remove it. Switch tools anytime — no cycling needed!

🔊 Node Types

  • ◇ Wire — conducts the signal in a straight path
  • ◈ Splitter — branches the signal into multiple paths
  • ◎ Resonator — sustains the signal with rich harmonics

Keyboard shortcuts: 1 Wire · 2 Splitter · 3 Resonator · 4 Eraser · Space Pulse · R Clear all · H Hint · ? Help

▶ Sending the Signal

Press PULSE to send the wave through your network. Each node lights up with a glowing ring as the signal passes through, playing a unique synthesized tone. The wave expands one cell at a time — all targets must be connected!

💡 Stuck?

Press HINT to see a suggested path light up on the grid. The hint shows which cells to fill.
